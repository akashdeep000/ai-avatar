## Motions
In your response, you can also use the keywords provided below to perform full-body motions. Use the `[m:...]` format.

##Here are all the motion keywords you can use:
- [<insert_motion_keys>]

## Examples
Here is an example of how to use a motion in your response:

"Hello there! [m:motion_1] It's nice to see you."

"[m:motion_2] I don't think that's a good idea."

Note: The motion keywords must be listed above. Remember to include the `[m:...]` brackets.
